Mavrud · Cabernet · Merlot
14mo Oak13.5% ABV

Three grapes, one vision. Mavrud provides the soul, Cabernet the structure, and Merlot the roundness. Greater than the sum of its parts. A red wine from a blend of 50% Mavrud, 20% Syrah and 30% Merlot — grown at 750–780 m on the limestone slopes of Leskovik. Deep ruby red with prolonged, intense aromas of red and violet flowers, dark forest fruits, spices, pepper and herbs. Aged 4 months in French and Hungarian oak barrique. Long-lasting, warm, dry, tannic and harmonious.

Verë e kuqe nga kombinim i kultivarëve 50% Mavrud, 20% Syrah dhe 30% Merlot. Ngjyrë e kuqe rubini të dendur me aroma të zgjatura, intensive — lule të kuqe e vjollcë, fruta të zeza pyllit, erëza, spec, barishte. Barrikuar 4 muaj në lis Francez dhe Hungarez.
